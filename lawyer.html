About the Lawyer

Robert has over thirty years of experience assisting both large and small corporations and individuals through various immigration processes. Services include processing nonimmigrant visas (H-1B, H-1B1, E-3, L-1A, L-1B, TN, K-1, F-1), immigrant visas (first, second and third preference employment-based and relative family-based), naturalizations, deferred action applications, violence against women's act petitions and waivers of unlawful stays. He has represented many large corporations including Cisco Systems, Integrated Systems, Inc., Applied Materials, Fiserv, URS Corporation, Synapse Design Automation, and Jacobs Associates among others.

As a member of the nationally association of immigration lawyers, the American Immigration Lawyers Association and a founding member of the local Santa Clara Valley Chapter of the Association, Robert is actively involved in all aspects of immigration law. Robert attends both local and national conferences to stay up-to-date on recent developments in the law and in agency policies and has numerous resources to insure that he will be a strong advocate.

Robert also volunteers when time permits at various immigration-related low cost clinics and events to assist those who are unable to retain a strong advocate. All in all, Robert will strive to navigate your immigration case through the maze of rules and regulations to achieve your goals.
